About Us

HealthConnect represents our hospital and is a comprehensive hospital management system designed to streamline the operations of EWU Hospital. Our goal is to efficiently manage patient records, appointments, staff schedules, and medical inventories. This project has been developed to address the challenges faced by EWU Hospital due to the fragmented nature of their current systems, which hinder the synchronization of information and lead to operational inefficiencies.

Our system offers an integrated solution that ensures seamless communication and coordination across various departments within the hospital. By implementing HealthConnect, EWU Hospital can expect to see significant improvements in the accuracy of patient information, the efficiency of appointment scheduling, and the overall management of medical inventories.

Key features of HealthConnect include:

  • Electronic Health Records (EHR) management for secure and efficient patient information handling.
  • Appointment scheduling system that reduces wait times and improves patient satisfaction.
  • Staff scheduling module to optimize workforce management and ensure adequate staffing levels.
  • Inventory management system to track and manage medical supplies, reducing waste and ensuring availability.

HealthConnect is committed to enhancing the operational efficiency of EWU Hospital, providing a robust platform that supports the hospital's mission of delivering high-quality healthcare services to its patients.
